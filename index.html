Introduction

404-Squad is a group of 10 Final Year students pursuing Computer Science from University Of Karachi. We are currently working on a project for the course "Web Development".


As part of the BSCS 633 Internet Application Development course, a challenge was held between two groups of 10 members each. Each team selected a problem from the first four chapters of Jeffrey C. Jackson's *Web Technologies* book and presented it as a challenge for the other group to solve.
